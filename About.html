About Us

Our mission at Erickson is to provide a high level of service, diverse selection of quality products, and supply in a timely fashion which allows our customers the best possible advantage in a competitive marketplace.

Erickson Distribution, established in 1999, remains a privately owned distribution company whose main niche in the Canadian market place, which differentiates itself from the larger national distributors in Canada, is value added product selection, knowledgeable sales staff and a level of service that is unrivaled in our marketplace.

Erickson Distribution offers several different vendor product lines geared towards specialty products found in the home. Erickson's core product lines consist of wood staircase systems, iron staircase systems, fiberglass columns, wood columns, exterior railing systems and porch posts. Over the past 5 years, Erickson Distribution has successfully developed and continued service to over 700 retail customers; consisting of lumberyards, hardware stores and specialized retail outlets in Ontario.
