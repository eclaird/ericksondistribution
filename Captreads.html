Key Features

Our False Stair Treads are perfect for the "Do-it Yourselfer"

They are a quick simple method to convert carpeted steps into an elegant wooden stairway.

No need to cut off the existing bullnose

Our treads and risers are designed to cap the existing stairs quickly with minimal preparation

Available in Oak and Maple.
